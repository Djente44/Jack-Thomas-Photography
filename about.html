About me

My name is Jack and I am from the North East of England. I am a man with many passions and way too many hobbies to keep track o4f.. I have always enjoyed photography but what I love the most about it is that it allows me to capture memories of the things I love doing the most.

What is this website for?

I am currently learning to code and want to become a better programmer. This website is a perfect opportunity for me to practice and to create somewhere permeanent to share my hobbies, passions with the people that care. It also gives me a reason to take good photographs and motivates me to curate the best selection of them.


I hope you enjoy this site and my photographs!
